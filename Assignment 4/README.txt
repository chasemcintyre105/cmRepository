Assignment 4: Terrain

COMP 437: Computer Game Development Spring 2018

Part 1 (40 points): Create a 2D or 3D terrain. It should be large enough to be explored by your player gameobject.

Part 2 (40 points): Use the terrain shaping tools to ensure that the terrain can be comfortably navigated with your player controller(s).

Part 3 (20 points): Texture or color the terrain.
