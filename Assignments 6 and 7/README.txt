Assignment 6 and 7: Intro to Blender

COMP 437: Computer Game Development Spring 2018

Part 1 (25 points): As a group, select an assortment of simple objects to model and paint. You must select at least as many objects as group members.

- Bishop
- King
- Knight
- Pawn
- Queen
- Rook

Part 2 (75 points): Model the objects using Blender.

Part 3 (50 points): Paint the models. You may unwrap them and paint, or simply color faces.

Part 4 (50 points): Import all painted models into a single scene in Unity, with some camera control scheme implemented. This scene is your assignment submission.
